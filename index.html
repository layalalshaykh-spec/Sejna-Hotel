Selamat datang · Welcome

Where the jungle breathes and the river sings

SENJA was built by hand from volcanic stone and reclaimed ironwood, folded into a terraced hillside above the sacred Ayung. Each villa opens to birdsong and mist; each evening closes with an offering of frangipani and the low sound of the gamelan drifting up the valley.

We keep only eighteen villas, so the forest stays louder than the footsteps.
